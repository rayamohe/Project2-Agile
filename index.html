Welcome to BCIT’s New Media Industry Internship Finder!

Here recent students or graduates can find their potential employer to gain new skills and on-the-job learning experience. The industry internship finder helps not only students but also industry employers to find potential candidates for their internship position.

The main focus of the industry internship finder is to aid students in finding an Internship and potentially their future employer. Finding an internship can be difficult, especially if you are new to it. “Where should I look? Where should I go? What is an internship exactly?”. An internship can come in many shapes, forms, and sizes. Some internships are paid or unpaid. Whatever the case is, an internship is an entry level job opportunity that will always provide you valuable learning experience while on-the-job. These experiences are important for not only learning but also for possible job opportunities in the future.

Here at BCIT we provide innovated programs that offer high-quality training and tools that helps students be job ready once graduated. We are fully confident in our New Media programs that are offered here at BCIT. We do believe industry employers will benefit from these students and what they have to offer. Employers can view each student’s profile to review their skill set and learning outcomes. Employers are also able to create a detailed listing of the internship position available, where students can review and apply.
